-- SONG POPULARITY ANALYSIS --

- This repository looks at a Kaggle dataset of over 30,000 songs,
- and uses methods such as Exploratory Data Analysis, Feature Engineering, and more
- in order to see genre specific trends among popular songs.

Genre Specific analysis uncovered trends such as
* Higher energy songs tend to be less popular overall
* Songs with more vocals compared to instrumentalness are more popular
* Danceability is genre specific - better for rap and pop
* liveness is the most important metric overall - songs are typically more popular with low liveness (positivity)
